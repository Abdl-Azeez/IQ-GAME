Steps to follow

From the Welcome page click the Get started button provided, then it's navigate you to the simple tutorial page

From the tutorial page simply click on Lets get started, and here you go, you can play the game, answer each question, provided you know the answer or not

On the questions page, click on your choosen option, then click the Next question button, continue the process till the whole question is finished

On the 10th question page, the Next question button become Finish button, simply because it is the last question, click on it to see the overview of your performance during the game

Viewing your performance, a Retry button is provided at the end of the page, feel free to retry as much as you can, their is also a button provided to view all the links of the questions for better explanation.
